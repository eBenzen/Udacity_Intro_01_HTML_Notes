Lesson 1 - Welcome to Nanodegree

Debugging
1. Collect evidence (what makes this program fail?)
2. Generate theories (what may have caused this problem?)
3. Test those theories (if my theory is correct, how could I find out?)
4. Fix the problem

Neat part on the fact that they are not your teachers, but rather you ultimately are always in control of the EDU. So in other words, do not judge and education program by how successful the teachers are at the material, but rather how they can equip you to learn master the subject yourself.
